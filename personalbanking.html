Savings Account

A savings account is the most fundamental type of account you can open at any bank. By definition, a savings account allows you to securely deposit your money with the bank, eliminating the need to carry cash or hide it in an old safe at home. You can easily withdraw these funds whenever necessary. Additionally, you will receive a cheque book, passbook, and ATM card for your convenience.

There are various types of savings accounts available, including traditional savings accounts, highyield savings accounts, money market accounts, certificates of deposit, cash management accounts, and specialty savings accounts. Our professional staff is dedicated to helping you choose the right option that fits your needs, ensuring you have the best experience possible. Plus, you’ll be pleased to know that the charges for maintaining a savings account are low, making it an affordable choice for managing your funds.

Current Account

A current account is a versatile bank account that allows you to store and withdraw money as needed. Most banks offer a variety of current accounts with different features, enabling you to find the account that best suits your requirements. You will also receive a cheque book and ATM card for easy access to your funds. Current accounts can be opened by individuals, partnership firms, private and public limited companies, HUFs, specified associations, societies, and trusts. The procedures for opening a current account are similar to those for savings accounts.
