Deposit Schemes

A deposit scheme provides a safe and effective way to save and grow your money, ensuring both security and steady returns. With the flexibility to choose from various deposit options, you can build financial stability and achieve your savings goals without the stress of market risks. At Secunderabad Bank, our deposit schemes are tailored to meet your needs, offering attractive interest rates and unmatched convenience.

Fixed Deposit

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a secure and reliable investment option that guarantees returns over a predetermined period. With an FD, you deposit a lump sum for a specific tenure at a fixed interest rate, ensuring you earn more than with a standard savings account. This investment comes without any market-related risks, making it an excellent choice for those seeking a stable and predictable way to grow their wealth. Whether you’re saving for future education, a major purchase, or just want to enjoy the peace of mind that comes with a secure investment, a Fixed Deposit can help you achieve your financial goals. With flexible tenure options and guaranteed returns, FDs offer both safety and financial growth. At Secunderabad Bank, we make it easy to invest in an FD with competitive interest rates and customer-focused service.

Recurring Deposit

A Recurring Deposit (RD) is the perfect solution for those who prefer to save consistently without requiring a large initial deposit. By contributing a fixed amount monthly over a set period, you can accumulate significant savings while earning assured returns at maturity. Ranging from short to long tenures, RDs offer the flexibility to align your savings with specific goals—whether it’s planning a vacation, funding education, or simply building a disciplined habit of saving. The predictable nature of RDs makes them ideal for individuals seeking financial stability without market volatility. At Secunderabad Bank, our RDs provide the security of fixed returns, ensuring your hard-earned money grows steadily over time.

Re-Investment Policy

For those looking to maximize the potential of their Fixed Deposits, the Re-Investment Policy offers a powerful way to grow wealth. This strategy adds the interest earned on your FD back to the principal amount on a quarterly basis, enabling you to benefit from the power of compounding. Over time, this compounding effect results in significantly higher returns, making it an excellent choice for long-term savings. By choosing the Re-Investment Policy, you can watch your money grow exponentially without any additional effort. Whether you’re saving for retirement, higher education, or simply want to build a substantial financial corpus, our Re-Investment Policy ensures your financial future is both secure and rewarding.

Daily Deposit

The Daily Deposit Scheme is a unique offering tailored for individuals with variable incomes, such as small business owners, shopkeepers, and self-employed professionals. This scheme allows you to deposit small amounts daily, gradually building a substantial savings corpus over time. With attractive interest rates and the flexibility to withdraw after six months, it’s a practical solution for those looking to grow their savings steadily.
