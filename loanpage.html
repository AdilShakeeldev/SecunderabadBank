Loans & Advances

Loans and advances are funds provided by banks, requiring repayment. They include options like secured or unsecured loans and fixed or variable rates. Advances cater to short-term needs, repaid within a year, while loans serve long-term financing.

Both involve interest and can be repaid in installments or as a lump sum. At Secunderabad Bank, we help you fulfill your financial needs with flexible lending solutions tailored to your goals!

Gold Loan

A Gold Loan allows you to borrow funds by pledging your gold jewelry as collateral, providing quick access to cash with minimal documentation and competitive interest rates. While other companies may advertise rates below 1% but include hidden charges that raise the cost to 3%, we offer a straightforward rate of just 1% with no hidden fees.

Enjoy the convenience of immediate appraisal, with loan sanctioning within 15 minutes after due process. Secure the highest value for your jewelry and meet your financial needs without selling your valuable assets. Get your gold loan in just 15 minutes!

Vehicle Loan

A Vehicle Loan makes owning your dream car or two-wheeler more affordable and accessible. We customize the loan amount, tenure, and interest rates to suit your financial profile, ensuring flexible repayment options that work for you.

With fast approvals and minimal paperwork, you can hit the road in no time. Enjoy some of the best low-interest rates available and let us be your partner in the purchase. Get your dream vehicle with us today!

Loan on Deposit

A Loan on Deposit allows you to borrow against your Fixed Deposit, giving you access to funds without breaking your savings. Instead of forgoing interest on your deposit, you can obtain a loan equivalent to a percentage of your deposit amount while still benefiting from your interest earnings

This convenient solution offers quick liquidity, making it perfect for urgent needs while preserving your savings. With this option, you can keep earning from your deposits while accessing funds for shorter periods. Enjoy financial flexibility with Secunderabad Bank!
