Cash Credit

Cash Credit is a flexible short-term loan tailored to meet the working capital needs of businesses. It allows you to borrow funds against your inventory, receivables, or stock within a pre-sanctioned credit limit. Withdraw as required, and pay interest only on the amount used—not the entire credit line.

With Cash Credit from Secunderabad Bank, you get the liquidity to manage day-to-day operations, seasonal demands, and business growth—all while optimizing your cash flow. Our team offers expert guidance to help you set up the most effective facility for your business.

Over Draft

An Over Draft facility offers instant access to funds beyond your account balance, giving you the power to manage short-term financial needs with ease. Whether it's an emergency, unexpected bill, or business requirement, an overdraft gives you breathing space without requiring a new loan.

Interest is charged only on the drawn amount, and repayments are flexible, helping you stay financially agile. With interest rates between 8% and 19%, Secunderabad Bank's Over Draft service is designed for both individuals and businesses seeking temporary financial backup.
