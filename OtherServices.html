RTGS / NEFT

Enjoy the convenience of real-time and scheduled fund transfers with our RTGS (Real-Time Gross Settlement) and NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer) services. Whether you need to send money urgently or plan regular payments, our services guarantee secure and efficient transfers to any bank across India. With a user-friendly process and real-time updates, managing your finances has never been easier. Trust Secunderabad Bank to make your fund transfers fast, reliable, and stress-free.

  • IFSC Code: SVCB0029002
  • Secure transactions with multi-layer authentication
